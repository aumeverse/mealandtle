Oral Health Do's & Don'ts

Expert tips from our dentists

The Do's

  • Brush & Floss Your Teeth Daily
  • Visit Your Dentist Regularly
  • Practice Good Brushing Technique
  • Drink Water & Rinse After Meals
  • Eat Fresh Vegetables & Fruits

The Don'ts

  • Avoid Smoking & Tobacco
  • Avoid Eating Ice Cubes
  • Avoid Using Toothpicks Harshly
  • Avoid Eating Sticky Snacks & Candy
  • Avoid Excessive Use of Teeth (e.g., opening bottles)
